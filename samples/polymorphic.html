This sample is a document redesign in which I changed the medium from a long-form academic essay into a visually structured website. I used design principles to structure the information to guide readers through complex material and create a more engaging and user-friendly experience for the audience. I decided to include this sample to demonstrate my skills in document design and my ability to adapt content for different formats while maintaining clarity and effectiveness.
