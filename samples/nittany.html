Reflection

This writing sample is the result of a group project I helped complete for my Networking and Telecommunications course. Our team was tasked with designing a wide area network (WAN) for a company with several locations around Centre County, Pennsylvania. My primary role in the project was to research the security requirements for the network and choose appropriate technologies to meet those needs. I also contributed to the overall network design and documentation. I included this sample to demonstrate my ability to work collaboratively on technical documents, my understanding of network design principles. and my security focus.
